Biography:

Minnie Mouse is an animated, anthropomophic charracter, created by the
greats Ub Iwerks and Walt Disney by 1928. Minnie is known to be the
girlfriend to Micky Mouse, and a close friend to Daisy Duck and a friend
to Clarabelle Cow. Minnie is classy, cheerful, and feminine. She is so
kind, and careful that is why we all loved her as kids.
